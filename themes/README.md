# out-of-the-box themes

## default

*   **theme description:** the default theme for the discerning hacker.
*   **colors:** a classic cyberpunk palette of blues, greys, and white.
*   **characters:** standard ascii.

## runner

*   **theme description:** "all those moments will be lost in time, like tears in rain."
*   **colors:** the cold, blue hues of a dystopian los angeles.
*   **characters:** standard ascii.

## blade

*   **theme description:** "it's too bad she won't live, but then again who does?"
*   **colors:** the same cold blues of the runner theme, but with a splash of defiant red.
*   **characters:** standard ascii.

## matrix

*   **theme description:** "i know kung fu."
*   **colors:** the iconic green and black of the matrix.
*   **characters:** a cascade of alphanumeric characters.

## metro

*   **theme description:** "the mediator between head and hands must be the heart!"
*   **colors:** the stark, industrial greyscale of a futuristic metropolis.
*   **characters:** the cold, unfeeling binary of the machine.

## johnny

*   **theme description:** "i want to get online... i need a computer!"
*   **colors:** the bright, electric turquoise of a mind on fire.
*   **characters:** the raw data of the net, represented in binary.

## akira

*   **theme description:** "the future is not a straight line. it is filled with many crossroads."
*   **colors:** the vibrant, chaotic neons of neo-tokyo.
*   **characters:** the sharp, angular lines of japanese katakana.

## ghost

*   **theme description:** "what if a cyber brain could possibly generate its own ghost, create a soul for itself? and if it did, just what would be the importance of being human then?"
*   **colors:** the cool, detached blues and purples of a cybernetic world, with a hint of human warmth in the red.
*   **characters:** a mix of alphanumeric and abstract symbols, representing the ghost in the machine.

## dark

*   **theme description:** "you know, i have the strangest feeling i've been here before."
*   **colors:** the oppressive, sickly yellows and greens of a city with no sun.
*   **characters:** a distorted and unsettling mix of alphanumeric characters and strange symbols.

## stalker

*   **theme description:** "the zone is a very complicated system of traps, and they're all deadly."
*   **colors:** the muted, earthy greens and browns of a world reclaimed by nature.
*   **characters:** the cyrillic alphabet, a reminder of the zone's mysterious origins.
